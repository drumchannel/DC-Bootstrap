HLAG 2016 Roundtable Discussion Part 1

Roundtable Discussion

Join Kimberly Thompson, Val Sepulveda, Alicia Warrington, Sam Maloney and Kiran Ghandi for a great panel discussion regarding their lives and musical careers. You will learn a lot about their experiences, history, and personal insights into the music industry.

The panelists introduce themselves and discuss their backgrounds. You will learn about how they came to become drummers as well as the experiences that have led them to where they are in this point of their careers.
