Rick Latham's

Serious Shuffles

Master Class

Rick Latham has played with some of the biggest names in the music industry. His credits range from Edgar Winter to Stevie Ray Vaughan. In this series of lessons, he will show you different stylistic interpretations of the shuffle, as well as exercises to improve your groove and coordination as it relates to the shuffle.

Secrets From the Pros

Short on time? Secrets from the pros are short, “micro” lessons designed for maximum impact and information in as little amount of time as possible. (Typically 2-10 minutes)

Master Classes

Looking to dive deeper? Masterclasses are a series of 5-15 min lessons on one specific topic or concept bundled together for a totally immersive experience. (Released periodically.)
