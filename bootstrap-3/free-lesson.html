The Paradiddle-diddle

Instructor: Dave Elitch

Dave Elitch has played with The Mars Volta, Miley Cyrus, and many others. He is known in the music industry as a highly versatile and musical drummer.

The paradiddlediddle is a simple rudiment in its base form. One pair of singles followed by two pairs of doubles. The way you play the doubles is very important.

Difficulty: Beginner  3:36
