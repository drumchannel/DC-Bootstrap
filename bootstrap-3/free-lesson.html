Breakdown of Meters Songs

Instructor: Zigaboo Modeliste

Zigaboo is the founding drummer of the legendary funk group The Meters. His take on New Orleans drumming has innovated pop music and modern funk. Along with his extensive songwriting background, Zigaboo has become a prominent figure in pop music. And if that weren't enough, he was recently voted into the Rolling Stone list of the top 100 drummers of all time!

Zig talks about his approach to drumset and breaks down one of the grooves in The Meters’ songs, “Jungle Man.” He also talks about how he composes his fills and keeps everything grooving.

Difficulty: All Levels  8:28

His recordings with The Meters are legendary. Being funky is one of the biggest compliments a drummer can receive and Zig is the master. This DVD will be one of the most important lessons of your drumming career.
