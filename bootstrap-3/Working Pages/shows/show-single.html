Part 1 - Everything you Need to Know About Berklee College of Music

John describes his duties as Chair of the percussion department at Berklee College of Music and the audition requirements. He also describes his upcoming Drum Channel course that will prepare you to pass your audition. He discusses picking your major and the 6 percussion instruments you can choose from: drum set, vibraphone, marimba, hand percussion (Afro-Cuban, Brazilian, West African, Middle Eastern), orchestral percussion, and steel pan.

Coming in December John Ramsay's complete course will teach you everything you need to know to pass your Drum set audition. He covers Reading, Rudiments, Song Form, Time Feel and your Prepared Piece.
