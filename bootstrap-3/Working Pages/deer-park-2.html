Deer Park Avenue | Part 2

Deer Park Avenue is a rock band founded by Sarah and Stephanie Snyder, sisters who started out as journalists and now are musicians, music bloggers, and voting members of the Recording Academy (Grammy awards). They came to the DC Studios for a special band performance.

Deer Park Avenue was the winner of Hard Rock Rising (Washington, D.C.) battle of the bands. Their self-titled debut album was released worldwide in June 2016, after a PledgeMusic campaign that let fans go behind the scenes of the making of the new album. They worked with renowned producer and bassist Matt Bissonette, known for his work with Elton John, Brian Wilson, Rick Springfield, Don Henley, and many others. Their track “Countdown” features Grammy Award-winning drummer Gregg Bissonette (David Lee Roth, Santana, Ringo Starr) playing double drums with Stephanie. "Deer Park Avenue" is available now on iTunes, Amazon, and Spotify. Get more info at deerparkave.com/bio.
