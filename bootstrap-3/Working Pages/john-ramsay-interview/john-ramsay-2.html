Part 2 - Everything you Need to Know About the Berklee Audition

John discusses the 5 areas for the admissions auditions, as well as the placement audition. He breaks down what you should do and what they are looking for. He also discusses Alan Dawson’s Rudimental Ritual and his book, The Drummer’s Complete Vocabulary As Taught by Alan Dawson.

Coming in December John Ramsay's complete course will teach you everything you need to know to pass your Drum set audition. He covers Reading, Rudiments, Song Form, Time Feel and your Prepared Piece.
