Learn to Play Drums in 10 minutes with Val Sepulveda, Chad Smith, Greg Garman and Don Lombardi


Val Sepulveda is the winner of the 2013 Hit Like a Girl Contest.  She joined us in the studio for a series of beginner lessons aimed at getting you started on the drum set quickly!

In this lesson, she will discuss and show you the first basic rock beat that you will learn in this series.  She is joined by some special guests as well!
