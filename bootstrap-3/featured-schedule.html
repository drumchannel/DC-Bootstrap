 Congratulations!

Legendary drummer of iconic rock band Chicago Danny Seraphine inducted into the Rock N' Roll Hall of Fame!

Pioneering jazz-rock drummer/composer/author and founding member of the Grammy® Award-winning rock group, Chicago, Danny Seraphine was officially inducted into the Rock n’ Roll Hall of Fame on Friday, April 8th at Brooklyn’s Barclay’s Center. Worldwide, Chicago has sold in excess of 122 million albums and has earned an estimated 22 gold, 18 platinum and 8 multiplatinum albums. Danny is a great inspiration to all drummers and the Drum Channel family want to extend our heartfelt congratulations to him! If you haven't already seen his dvd, check it out in the link below!

37 - Double Drag Paradiddle

Instructor: Adam Kuns

Building off of the single drag paradiddle, we make it into an 8-stroke phrase. It switches hands with each repetition.

Master Class

Tuesday: 4/12

38 - Single Ratamacue

Instructor: Adam Kuns

The ratamaue is a great combination of hand dexterity and motion. We practice our single stroke four (4-stroke ruff) motion while adding a ruff to the downbeat.

Master Class

Wednesday: 4/13

39 - Double Ratamacue

Instructor: Adam Kuns

The double ratamacue shifts the rudiment into a three-note phrase. Two sets of ruffs and the four stroke motion for the ratamacue.

Master Class

Thursday: 4/14

40 - Triple Ratamacue

Instructor: Adam Kuns

The triple ratamacue shifts the rudiment one more time to a four-note phrase with three sets of ruffs. Congratulations! By the time you finish this one, you will have completed the rudimental crash course.
