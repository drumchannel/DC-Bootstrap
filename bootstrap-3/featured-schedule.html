Rick Latham's

Serious Shuffles

Master Class

Rick Latham has played with some of the biggest names in the music industry. His credits range from Edgar Winter to Stevie Ray Vaughan. In this series of lessons, he will show you different stylistic interpretations of the shuffle, as well as exercises to improve your groove and coordination as it relates to the shuffle.

 House Instructor Lessons

Monday: 2/8

1 - Single Stroke Roll

Instructor: Adam Kuns

In this series of lessons, we will be learning our 40 standard rudiments! Beginning with the fundamental single stroke roll.

Master Class

Tuesday: 2/9

2 - Single Stroke Four

Instructor: Adam Kuns

We will add the concept of space to the single stroke roll, which will allow us to begin working on the mechanics of motion for our single stroke roll.

Master Class

Wednesday: 2/10

3 - Single Stroke Seven

Instructor: Adam Kuns

We continue to the single stroke seven which allows more of the repetitive motion in the single stroke so we can begin to build up the feeling of relaxation in the hands.

Master Class

Thursday: 2/11

4 - Buzz Roll

Instructor: Adam Kuns

This lesson introduces the idea of adding pressure to the fulcrum to create multiple bounce notes with each stroke. This will eventually develop into the double stroke roll.
