Pete Lockett - Indian Rhythms and Percussion

Instructor: Pete Lockett

In this master class, Pete Lockett will take us out of the comfort zone of western musical tradition. We will examine Indian rhythms and musical structure to see how they apply their techniques in the context of Indian tradition, and will eventually come back and apply it with a more Western approach.

Difficulty: Advanced  12 Lessons
