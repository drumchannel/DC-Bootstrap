Secrets From the Pros Review Exercises

Let's take a look at some methodical methods to approach the recent Secrets From the Pros from Raul Pineda, Joey Peebles and Mike Clark.

Cobus demonstrates a groove and fill that both utilize the inverted paradiddle. This rudiment is a great alternative to the standard paradiddle and offers its own unique voicings and feel to grooves and fills as well as soloing.
Improve 16th Note and Triplet Feel

Just off the road with James Taylor, Chad explains being a good drummer and a great musician. He discusses playing musically and recognizing what the song needs from you vs playing what your ego wants to hear. A lesson all drummers should be aware of!

Paradiddle Accents

Wrap your brain around the paradiddle and its various inversions. This exercise will engage all four limbs as an independence exercise as well as a stick exercise. It will improve your flow around the kit and expand your vocabulary. Once you've mastered these exercises you can apply them to various scenarios and settings that call for them.
