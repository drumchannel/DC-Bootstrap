Attention Female Drummers Ages 13 and Up!

Win Drum Gear and Have Your Video Featured on Drum Channel!

Drum Channel and Hit Like A Girl are seeking drum videos for “The Beat”, a new show for and about female drummers. Videos can be originals or covers and should be around 3 minutes long. Submitted videos will be posted on Drum Channel’s YouTube channel and some will be featured and discussed on the show by a panel of top drummer/educators from Drum Channel, the Sabian Educator Network, Vic Firth’s Private Teacher program and the D’Addario Education Collective. Featured videos also receive drum gear and merch prizes. To participate, upload your video to YouTube or Vimeo and submit it in the form below. The goal of "The Beat" is to help you improve when you enter the Hit Like A Girl contest in February.
